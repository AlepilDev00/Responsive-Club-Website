About us

Games of the Week

Welcome to Sanctuary TTRPG, the ultimate destination for tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) enthusiasts! Whether you're a veteran adventurer or just starting your first campaign, our club provides the perfect space to roll dice, tell stories, and meet other passionate players. Through our online booking system, you can easily:

Join a Table: Reserve a seat in one of our ongoing or scheduled TTRPG sessions and play alongside new friends. Ideal for solo players looking to join a group.

Book a Private Table: Bring your own party and secure a table for your group's exclusive session.

Get Event Tickets: Stay up to date with our special events, themed nights, tournaments, and one-shots — and book your tickets directly through our site.

Our venue is designed to offer a welcoming, creative atmosphere with everything you need for an immersive TTRPG experience — from custom terrain and miniatures to snacks and drinks for adventurers of all alignments.

Join the community, explore new worlds, and let the dice decide your fate at Sanctuary TTRPG.

Dungeons & Dragons Night

At Sanctuary TTRPG, we believe tabletop roleplaying is more than just rolling dice — it's about connection, creativity, and shared moments of wonder. Join a table of fellow adventurers for an exclusive evening of Dungeons & Dragons, guided by a skilled Dungeon Master who will weave your choices into a living tale of courage, mystery, and magic.
Whether you're a first-time explorer or a battle-hardened veteran, this event is designed to welcome everyone. You'll laugh, strategize, and celebrate every triumph together as your group faces the unexpected twists of fate that only D&D can deliver.
